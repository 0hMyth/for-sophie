ON AIR
I

The Standard Bearer

You don't just play the game, you lead the line,

With a mind as sharp as any elven blade.

You want the sun and all the stars to shine,

On every single progress that we've made.

A "Monica" precision in your touch,

Setting the bar where only you can reach.

You care for those you love—perhaps too much—

By being the example that you preach.

II

The Natural Twenty

It's not just luck when you begin to roll,

It's the fire in your spirit and your eyes.

You give the quest your mind, your heart, your soul,

The smartest player underneath the skies.

You're competitive, a fierce and lovely light,

The one who keeps the party on the path.

You win the day and win the weary fight,

Then save us from the dragon's fiery wrath.

III

The Ocean in Your Gaze

They say the sea is deep and wild and blue,

But they haven't seen the way you look at me.

I'd drown within the salt and silk of you,

And sink into that vast, cerulean sea.

Your eyes are maps to worlds I've never known,

A sapphire glow that guides the traveler home.

The brightest gems the earth has ever shown,

Beneath the rolling surf and white-capped foam.

IV

The Softness of the Clouds

Your smile could break the winter's coldest spell,

A flash of light that makes the world feel right.

And of your lips, there's much for me to tell—

As soft as clouds that drift in pale moonlight.

Between the honey and the forest floor,

Between the clever wit and grand design,

It's your comfort that I'm always longing for,

The gentle way your hand is held in mine.

V

The Honey and the Hive

You gather sweetness like a summer bee,

But share it with the ones you hold most dear.

A selfless kind of "Golden Energy,"

That settles every doubt and every fear.

For family and friends, you are the shield,

The one who shows us how we ought to live.

To your compassion, every heart will yield,

For you have so much brilliance left to give.

VI

The Final Script

So here's to Sophie—brilliant, bold, and kind,

The "Legend-dary" girl who took the lead.

The greatest soul that I could hope to find,

The only hero that I'll ever need.

We'll finish every season, side by side,

From the mushroom groves to the starry blue.

With nothing left to fear and naught to hide,

I'm "I'll be there for you"—and only you.
